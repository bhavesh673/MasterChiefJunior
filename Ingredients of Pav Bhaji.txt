
Ingredients of Pav Bhaji

1) 4 Servings.
2) 4 medium mashed,boiled potato.
3) 2 medium chopped onion.
4) 1/2 cup chopped cauliflower.
5) 1 cup shelled,blanched peas.
6) 3 tablespoon refined oil.
7) salt as required.
8) 8 pav.
9) 2 teaspoon garlic paste.
10) 1/4 cup green beans.
11) 4 medium chopped tomato.
12) 1 chopped,de seeded capsicum ( green pepper).
13) 1/4 cup chopped carrot.
14) 1/2 tablespoon ginger paste.
15) 2 chopped green chilli.
16) 25 gm melted butter.
17) 1 medium lemon wedges.
18) 2 teaspoon pav bhaji masala.
